Core Points — quick takeaways

  • Menorah / Lampstand — God's light and the sevenfold Spirit; central shaft represents God's righteousness.
  • Two Olive Trees — continuous anointing and provision for the Two Witnesses.
  • The Living Word — Hebrews 4:12: the Word pierces and is the decisive power in transformation.
  • Practical call — worship, witness, and mercy as tangible responses to the vision.
"Not by might, nor by power, but by my Spirit." (Zechariah 4:6)
Full Teaching & References (expand/collapse)

(Expanded teaching available on the Teachings page — ideal for sharing with groups or printing.)

The Menorah is the picture of the symbol of the nation of Israel. It is the subliminal revelation of the fullness of the Creator God, His oneness, His threefold being, and His seven Spirits manifested in light.

The ascending and descending light reveals salvation — see Zechariah 4 and Revelation 11 for the scriptural anchor points.
