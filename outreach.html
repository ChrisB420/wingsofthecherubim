Wings of the Cherubim Outreach

Featuring EJIM: Encounter Jesus Intercessory Ministry

Mission

EJIM exists to intercede, pray, and reach communities with the love of Jesus. It operates as a key movement under Wings of the Cherubim, combining intercessory prayer with tangible outreach to transform lives spiritually and physically.

Activities

  • Intercessory prayer initiatives for families, individuals, and nations.
  • Community outreach and evangelism in Mombasa and surrounding regions.
  • Faith-based programs like T-shirt ministry to spread the Gospel.
  • Bible teaching seminars and mentorship for spiritual growth.
  • Support for local communities with tangible needs, aligned with Wings’ mission.

Updates & Transparency

EJIM reports regularly on its activities. We provide updates, photos, and reports from the field, ensuring that every contribution is used directly for ministry and outreach, impacting lives for Christ.
